QR CODE READER

Privacy Policy

Last Updated: August 1, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how QR Code Reader ("we," "us," or "our"), accessible from https://qrdecode.vinnesia.my.id, handles your information. Our service is a free, globally accessible tool that does not require user login or registration.

Your privacy is critically important to us. This policy is created in compliance with Indonesia's Law No. 27 of 2022 concerning Personal Data Protection (UU PDP) and other relevant international standards.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

We do not require you to create an account or provide any personally identifiable information (like name, email, or address) to use our service. Our core functionality is designed to be anonymous.

2. Data Processed by the Tool

When you use the QR Code Reader, you may provide data in the following ways:

  • Camera Access: If you grant permission, our tool uses your device's camera to scan QR codes. The camera stream is processed in real-time directly within your browser. We do not transmit, record, or store this video stream on our servers.
  • Image Uploads: If you upload an image containing a QR code, the image is processed locally in your browser to decode the information. The image file is not uploaded to or stored on our servers.

All QR code processing happens on the client-side (your device). We have no access to the images you scan or the data contained within your QR codes.

3. Information Collected Automatically (Non-Personal Data)

To improve our service and understand usage patterns, we use Google Analytics. This service collects anonymous, aggregate data, such as:

  • Your device type and browser
  • Your country and language settings
  • The pages you visit on our site and the time you spend on them
  • General usage metrics (e.g., whether the camera or upload feature was used)

This data is anonymized and cannot be used to identify you personally. It helps us optimize the website for different devices and regions.

4. Cookies and Third-Party Advertising

We may use cookies for essential site functionality and analytics. Additionally, we may participate in the Google AdSense program to display ads, which helps keep our service free. Here's how it affects you:

  • Google, as a third-party vendor, uses cookies (like the DART cookie) to serve ads based on a user's prior visits to our site and other sites on the Internet.
  • You may opt out of the use of the DART cookie by visiting the Google Ad Settings page.
  • For more information, please review Google's Privacy Policy.

5. Data Security

While we do not store personal data, we implement appropriate security measures to protect our website and the integrity of our service from unauthorized access or disruption.

6. Your Rights

Under Indonesian law and other data protection regulations, you have rights regarding your data. Since we do not collect personal information, these rights are primarily related to your browsing experience (e.g., managing cookies through your browser settings).

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: privacy@vinnesia.my.id.
