How to Use QR Code Reader

Our tool is designed to be simple and intuitive. You have two easy ways to scan a QR code: by uploading an image or by using your device's camera.

Method 1: Scanning a QR Code from an Image

Use this method if you have a QR code saved as an image file (e.g., .png, .jpg) on your computer or phone.

  1. On the homepage, locate the scanner box.
  2. Click the Choose Image File button. This will open your device's file explorer.
  3. Navigate to and select the image file containing the QR code.
  4. The tool will automatically process the image. The decoded information (like a URL, text, or contact info) will instantly appear in the "Scan Result" box.

Method 2: Scanning a QR Code with Your Camera

Use this method to scan a physical QR code in real-time.

  1. On the homepage, click the Start Camera button.
  2. Your browser will ask for permission to access your camera. You must click "Allow" for the scanner to work. We do not record or store your camera feed.
  3. Once the camera is active, simply point it at the QR code you wish to scan. Make sure the QR code is well-lit and fully visible within the scanner's viewfinder.
  4. The tool will automatically detect and decode the QR code. The result will appear in the "Scan Result" box.
  5. When you are finished, click the Stop Camera button to turn off the camera feed.

Troubleshooting Tips

  • Scan Fails: Ensure the QR code is clear and not blurry. Good lighting is important. Try moving your camera closer or further away.
  • Camera Not Working: Make sure you have granted camera permissions to your browser for our site. Check if another application is already using your camera.
  • No QR Code Found in Image: The image might be too low-resolution, or the QR code might be obstructed or damaged.
